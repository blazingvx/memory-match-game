    How to Play

    Welcome to the Memory Match Game! The objective is to find and match pairs of cards.

    Instructions:

    • Click on a card to flip it over and reveal its letter.
    • Try to remember the positions of the letters.
    • Click on another card to flip it over.
    • If the two cards match, they will stay flipped over.
    • If they don't match, they will flip back after a short delay.
    • Complete the level by matching all pairs of cards.
    • With each level, the number of pairs increases (up to 26 pairs).
    • Your time is recorded, and the leaderboard will display the top 10 fastest times.

    Good luck!
